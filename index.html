Games For Everyone

I like to focus on games & ideas that have universal appeal. This means that while designing a game, It should distinctly appeal to kids & adults. Can this be done perfectly? No. But it's something i like to work towards. Think about how you can watch a Disney animated movie as a kid as well as an adult.

Nostalgia

There are many trends in game apps that I dislike. Unfortunately due to mass adoption, These trends are here to stay. I want to use my spare time to bring back some old school gaming nostalgia. Hard levels, simple graphics, & quick load times which everyone can enjoy.

High Quality

I believe in making quality software that is robust and works not only on flagship devices, But on budget smartphones. This means writing clean optimized code and packaging it to be compatible with all devices.
